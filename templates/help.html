Command Center: Intel Guide

Welcome to the Recon & Draft Coach help portal. Use the sections below to understand our data-driven insights.

1. Understanding "Objective DNA"

Our radar charts visualize a team's priority. A "spiky" graph toward First Blood indicates a team that relies on early lane dominance, while a pull toward Dragons suggests a late-game scaling strategy.

2. Side Selection Logic

We analyze the "Blue vs Red" win-rate delta. If you see a warning for Red Side Vulnerability, it means the opponent struggles when they lose First Pick priority or fails to utilize the Counter-pick effectively.

3. "How to Win" Recommendations

These are generated via our Heuristic Engine. It cross-references the current game patch (e.g., v16.1) with historical performance to identify which players are currently "under-performing" on the meta picks.
